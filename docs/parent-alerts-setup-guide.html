Part 1 · Getting families on

Three ways in — all free

Each family joins once, then gets everything you send. The app is optional — messages arrive as text and email either way.

  1. Easiest: they sign up on the website

    Right after a parent submits the sign-up form, a “Turn on phone alerts” button appears. One tap adds them to your Remind class — no extra step for you.

  2. Or they join directly

    Great for grandparents, carpool drivers, or helpers. They text @6ee4bkk to 81010, or open remind.com/join/6ee4bkk.

  3. Or you add them yourself

    In Remind, use Add people and paste in phone numbers or emails. In your coach dashboard, Message families ▸ Copy phone numbers / Copy emails hands you that list ready to paste.

✅
No cost, no app required. Everyone who joins gets your messages as a phone notification, a text, and an email — Remind is free and there’s no per-text charge.
Part 2 · Send a message — ~20 sec

Reach every family at once

A rain-out, a field change, a reminder — same quick routine every time.

  1. Open Remind & pick your class

    Go to remind.com (or in the coach dashboard: Message families ▸ Open Remind to send) and choose LB Soccer Academy.

  2. Type it — flag it urgent if needed

    Write your note. For weather, cancellations, or safety, mark it urgent so it pushes through loud.

    Grass field is soaked — we’re on the turf tonight, same 6:00 start. See you there!

  3. Send — that’s it

    It goes to every joined family at once, by phone push, text, and email. The family that joined this morning is already included.

    🚨
    For emergencies, use Remind’s urgent option — it’s built to break through Do-Not-Disturb for weather and cancellations.
